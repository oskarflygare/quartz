### Key takeaways
The basic system is that you create up to 10 areas with 10 sub-folders each. They look like this, for example:

10-19 Finances
30-39 Hobbies
	- 30 Gaming
	- 31 Music
	- 32 Art
	- 34 Reading
	- 36 Travel
		36.01 Italy 2015
		36.02 Italy 2016
		...
40-49 Professional
50-59 Personal

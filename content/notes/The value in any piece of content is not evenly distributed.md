# The value in any piece of content is not evenly distributed
Date created: 2022-07-06

This is why you should try [[Note capture should focus on what resonates]], rather than copying down stuff word-for-word. You want the highest possible [[Signal versus noise]] ratio in your PKM.

>  in any piece of content, the value is not evenly distributed. There are always certain parts that are especially interesting, helpful, or valuable to you. When you realize this, the answer is obvious. You can extract only the most salient, relevant, rich material and save it as a succinct note.
